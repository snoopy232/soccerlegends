Mesut Özil

    • Biography

    Mesut Ozil is a former soccer player, who had many great achievements throughout his years. Some of his great achievements include winning the world cup, and FA Cup with Germany and Arsenal. Others include winning the La Liga, and winning german champion, with team Werder Bremen. Mesut Ozil is considered by many as the best playmaker, or number 10 in modern day soccer. Altough ozil had a career full of impressive solo achievements, aslong with his team achievements, he had an argument with the coach at arsenal, which led him to be thrown out by the club. He then joined the turkish side Istanbul Basaksehir were he would retire at 35, in the 22/23 season.

    Notable Contributions:

    • 2014 - Helped win Germany win the World Cup.
