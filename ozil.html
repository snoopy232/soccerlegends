Fernando Torres

Primarily played for Atletico Madrid, and Liverpool.
Played in the Athletico Madrid academy

Biography

Fernando Torres is a former soccer player with many great achievements throughout his years. His great achievements include winning the World Cup and two European championships with Spain. Others include winning the champions league, and 3 domestic english cups with Chelsea. Torres is widely considered as a "what if " player. Constant injuries plagued his career, which led him to retire at 35, in the 18/19 season.

Notable Contributions:

    2008,2012 - Helped win Spain the Euros.
  • 2010 - Helped Spain win the World Cup.
  • 2011/2012 - Won Chelsea the Champions League, and FA Cup

Famous Quote:
