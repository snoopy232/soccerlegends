Fernando Torres

Early Career

  • Primarily played for Atheletico Madrid, and Liverpool.
  • Played in the Athletico Madrid academy

    • Biography

    Fernando Torres is a former soccer player, who had many great achievements throughout his years. Some of his great achievements include winning the world cup, and two european championships with Spain. Others include winning the champions league, and 3 domestic english cups with Chelsea. Torres is widely considered as a "what if " player. Constant injuries plagued his career, which led him to retire at 35, in the 18/19 season.

    Notable Contributions:

    • 2008,2012 - Helped win Spain the Euros.
    • 2010 - Helped win Spain win the World Cup.
    • 2011/2012 - Won chelsea the Champions League, and FA cup

    Famous Quote:

    "You have to prove your self everyday"
