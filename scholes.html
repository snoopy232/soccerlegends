    • Biography

    Paul Scholes is a former soccer player, who had many great achievements throughout his years. Some of his great achievements include winning the Premier League 11 times, with Manchester United, and winning the Champions League with Manchester United twice in the 07/08 and 98/99 season. Paul Scholes is known for being one of the best midfielders of all time, playing for Manchester United, and England his whole career. His career was never dry of trophies, with him winning a total of 30 trophies in his long successful career. He retired playing for Manchester United, at the 12/13 he retired at the age of 38.

    Notable Contributions:

    • 2008,1999 - Champions league winner.
    • 1995/1996, 1996/1997, 1998/1999, 1998/2000, 2000/2001, 2002/2003, 2006/2007, 2007/2008, 2008/2009, 2010/2011, 2012/2013 - Premier League winner
    • 1998/1999 - Won the Treble for Manchester United, Winning the Premier League, FA cup and the Champions league.
